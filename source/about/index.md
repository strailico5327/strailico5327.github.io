<br><p>A plain boy from a coastal county in China, defined by cycling, doujin music, technology, Blender CG, linguistics and music games—with a touch of ACG and the love of his family and companions, a Christian. Though he believes that his level of mastery doesn’t determine his love for his hobbies, he doubts himself very often since he just *likes* them. 

Now he is studying Education at university and wishes to become a geography teacher at his former high school.

*Shards* was a novel written in high school as an escape for those stressful years, and now he picks it up and wishes to finish it one day.
